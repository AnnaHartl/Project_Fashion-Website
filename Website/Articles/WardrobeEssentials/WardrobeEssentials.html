Wardrobe Essentials

by Anna Hartl

These wardrobe essentials are the best way to building your own wardrobe and style. The point of having this certain things is that they can be easily combined with the new things you get to develop your style. It is also better for the environment, because you will end up in the long term buying less unesserary clothes.


Little black dress


It can go with every occasion, it is formal so a great piece for work, an office party or family event. It can be worn in the day with casual accessories or in the night as a evening dress. You can combine black with every possible color, I recommend either staying in the color black or using only ONE color.


Jeans


The most basic item everyone should have is a blue jeans and also a black one for a more polished look. They can be combined with everything, you can make an outfit from street look to classic, nearly everything is possible.


White t-shirt


A popular choice to a jeans is a white t-shirt. It is just something so simple but looks so put together. But a white t-shirt goes with basically everything. And it does not have to be all white, in trend right now are statement shirt.


Skirt


To spice up your wardrobe adding a skirt makes such a difference. It can be a short skirt in cord or a knee-length pleated one. It matches perfectly with the white t-shirt from before. Only having one skirt in your wardrobe gives you a lot more outfit choices in the morning.


Jeans Jacket


To add another layer on your outfit is a jeans jacket perfect. It is the best when you go outside and it is spring. Most people forget that the weather isn’t always on their side so to have a backup plane to keep you warm is essential.


A knitted sweater


This item is more for the winter months but nevertheless just a important then the rest of the cloths. Going with every occasion and every look a knitted sweater is a wardrobe essential for me. Makes your look more comfy but if you style it right you can look sophisticated.


Conclusion

Based on those wardrobe essential you can add different colors and styles as you desire. Having those basic items that go with nearly everything is the best way to save time in the morning when you stand in front of your closet and think you have nothing to wear. When you buy new clothes you have already thing to match. I always get bored with what I wear because I have my standard wardrobe these essentials make it easy to add to my basic outfits a new piece.
